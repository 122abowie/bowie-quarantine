Freehold High School

Freehold Boro High School is a place that may seem like just a school, but it is much more. It is a place where not only can you express yourself, but you can show who you are, without any fear of discrimination. Freehold Boro is more than just a school, its a family. Being a Colonel at Freehold Boro has taught me that school is more than just a place for learning, it is a place for development. It is a place where you can go to improve your intelligence and your life awareness. The amount of life skills that you can learn is incredible. The courses provided has a spectrum to tailor itself to your learning level. Teachers and staff are always there to help if you ever need it. To be a Freehold Boro Colonel means to be a family.
