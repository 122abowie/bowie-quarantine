Thank you to all First Responders and Healthcare workers

Dear workers, Thank you for risking your lives to help others in a time like this. It must be so hard going out every day to put your life
on the line for people you do not know during a pandemic that is affecting everyone. The importance of your work cannot be described by any words,
but to say the least each and every single one of you are absolute saviors. One day, the best in life will repay you as you are working on the front
lines and putting so much effort into helping others. Thank you all for being the best workers that you can be, and thank you all for donating so much time
and effort to all of your patients and the people that you surround yourself with. I know it must take a lot of time and effort to do what you all do, yet you
guys never let us down. You continue to show up to work and be the strong and resilient people that you are. You put your best efforts forward and you ensure
that your patients are not only healthy, but that they are happy as well. Thank you for being such kindhearted and strong individuals, and thank you for constantly
making sure that we all are doing alright. Thank you for working on the frontlines of this pandemic.
